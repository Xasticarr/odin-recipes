Slow Cooker Chop Suey

A tasty meal that only takes a short while to prepare, and then can be left to cook for several hours before enjoying

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Chop Suey Vegetables
  • 1 Can of Stir Fry Vegetables
  • 1 Packet of Stir Fry Seasoning
  • 1 lb Boneless Beef
  • 1 lb Boneless Pork
  • 2 Cans of Mushrooms
  • 1 Can of Water Chestnuts
  • 1 Can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Can of Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1/4 Cup of Soy Sauce
  • Rice Cooked For Serving

Steps

  1. Mix soups, soy sauce, and stir fry seasoning. Set aside mixture
  2. Cook Vegetables in slow cooker for 2 hours on high
  3. Cut both cuts of meat into cubes (sized to your preference)
  4. Brown both meats and drain
  5. Add meat and sauce mixture and cook for an additional 15 minutes
