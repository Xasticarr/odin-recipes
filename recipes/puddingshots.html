Pudding Shots

Pudding Shots of Any Flavor Desired

A simple, easy, and tasty dessert treat for adults to enjoy

Ingredients

  • 4.5 Cup Serving Pudding Mix
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Cup of Whipped Cream Vodka
  • 8 oz Light Cool Whip
  • 1 Package of 2oz Serving Cups

Steps

  1. Mix pudding packet and milk together in a medium sized mixing bowl until thickened
  2. Add vodka and whisk until smooth
  3. Add whipped cream and fold into mixture
  4. Stir gently until mixture is smooth
  5. Scoop mixture into 2oz serving cups
  6. Freeze pudding shots for ~4 hours or overnight
