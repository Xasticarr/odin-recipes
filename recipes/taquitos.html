Spicy Buffalo Chicken Taquitos

An easy to make appetizer or main dish depending on your preference

Ingredients

  • 1 Rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 Package of Cream Cheese (8oz), softened
  • 1/2 Cup of Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 Cup of Buffalo Sauce
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 24 Flour Tortillas, fajita sized
  • Non-stick Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°F
  2. Spray baking sheed with non-stick spray and set aside
  3. Mix softened cream cheese, ranch, and buffalo sauce in a large bowl until smooth
  4. Mix shredded chicken and cheese into cream cheese mixture
  5. For each taquito, spoon large tablespoon of mixture onto each tortilla in a thin line
  6. Roll tightly and place seam-down on baking sheet
  7. Repeat previous two steps until you've used up all of your mixture
  8. Once assembled, place all taquitos on baking sheet and generously spray the top of them with cooking spray
  9. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, flipping halfway through
  10. Cook until golden brown, then remove from oven to cool for 5 minutes
  11. Serve and enjoy
